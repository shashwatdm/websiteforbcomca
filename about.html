Bcom-Ca(computer application) Department

B.Com Computer Applications or Bachelor of Commerce in Computer Applications is an Under-Graduate Commerce course. Computer Applications are application software that is designed to help the user to perform specific tasks. The BCom Computer Applications is a degree that equips students with knowledge in both commerce as well as in computers. Its an under-graduation degree that will give you knowledge of the fundamentals of commerce along with an in-depth understanding of computer applications.
The Bachelor of Commerce in Computer Applications is generally a three academic year degree course and may also differ from place to place. The Bachelor of Commerce degree program in Computer Applications may be provided on a part-time basis by certain institutes. After completing the Bachelor of Commerce degree course candidates can find many career opportunities in different fields and its duration is three years
